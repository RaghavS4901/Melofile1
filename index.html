MeloFile
Good music exists in every inch of this country. It's just waiting to be found. So many ideas are wasted, and careers are cut short before they even begin just because musicians can't find people to play with. We're here to change that.
Melofile is a platform by and for musicians. It's about connecting individuals, building a scene where everybody who's willing has the means to make original music and tell their stories. Personal achievements are the assets that make up a movement, and our aim is to provide musicians with what they need to reach their goals. Building a directory is our first step towards that.

And you know what?

IT'S FREE FOR OUR FIRST 200 MEMBERS!

It's simple; sign up now and tell us about yourself through your profile so that like-minded players can connect with you. But be warned
-  it might get loud!
